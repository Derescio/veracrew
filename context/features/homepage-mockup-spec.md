# VeraCrew Homepage Mockup Spec

Create a high-converting SaaS marketing homepage for **VeraCrew** — a platform that helps field teams stay aligned, track operations, and maintain accurate real-time data.

**Output:** `prototypes/veracrew-homepage/` with:
- `index.html`
- `styles.css`
- `script.js`

---

## Brand Direction

**Core Message:**
"Run your crew with clarity, confidence, and real-time accuracy."

**Target Users:**
- Operations managers
- Field supervisors
- Construction / service teams
- Logistics coordinators

---

## Image Guidelines

- Field workers (construction, logistics)
- Teams collaborating
- Job site environments

Overlay UI elements for modern SaaS feel

---

## Conversion Strategy

This page should:
- Communicate value instantly
- Build trust quickly
- Guide users to CTA
- Emphasize clarity, control, and accuracy

Avoid:
- Too much text
- Weak CTAs
- Generic visuals
